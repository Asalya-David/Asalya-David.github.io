As already said in the History of the clan, there are currently 53 members present in the clan with an average age of 22 years. It is fully a Ghanaian clan but with a few people from other countries in Africa and beyond.

 

CLAN ADMINS

Just like any other organization, Exorcists also has a group of people who have the sole responsibility to make decisions on issues relating to Clan. Mickey, Detroit and Vade are responsible to the up running activities of the clan. Some of such responsibilities are;

1. Making the rules for the clan

2. Accepting new members and kicking out inactive members

3. Organize trainings and scrims to build up the clan's team work

4. Ensure that there is no bitterness between or among members in the clan

5. Handle the clan's social platforms such as Discord, WhatsApp, Youtube, Twitter, Instagram and the like.
